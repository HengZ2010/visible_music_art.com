Aviation Weather

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Aviation Weather does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require account registration, login, or personal details.

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Tracking data

2. Weather Data

The app retrieves publicly available aviation weather reports (such as METAR and TAF) from official aviation weather services. These requests are anonymous and contain no personal information.

3. Local Storage

Recent search history may be stored locally on your device for convenience. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally. Deleting the app will remove this data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses public aviation weather APIs to retrieve weather data. No personal user information is shared with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
