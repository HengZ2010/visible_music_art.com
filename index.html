一
Pitch → Color

Every frequency maps to its own hue across the full colour wheel — deep bass becomes violet and indigo, midrange blooms into green and gold, high harmonics catch amber and rose.

二
Volume → Form

Louder passages leave larger, more opaque marks. Quiet moments produce fine traces. The painting holds the full dynamic range of the song as texture and density.

三
Time → Space

The canvas reads left to right as a timeline. The beginning of a song paints the left edge; the end reaches the right. Every moment is permanently recorded in its place.
